Marinated Grilled Prawn
Ingredient
  • Garlic- 3 cloves, minced.
  • Olive oil- 1/3 cup.
  • Tomato sauce- 1/4 cup.
  • Red Wine Vinegar- 2 tablespoons.
  • Fresh Basil- 2 tablespoons chopped.
  • Salt- 1/2 teaspoon.
  • Cayenne Pepper- 1/4 teaspoon.
  • Fresh Prawn- 2 pounds, peeled.
  • Skewers
Methods
  • In a large bowl, stir together the garlic, olive oil, tomato sauce, and red wine vinegar. Season with basil, salt, and cayenne pepper. Add prawn to the bowl, and stir until evenly coated. Cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring once or twice.
  • Preheat grill for medium heat. Thread prawn onto skewers, piercing once near the tail and once near the head. Discard marinade.
  • Lightly oil grill grate. Cook prawn on preheated grill for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until opaque.
