Matar Paneer Recipe
Ingredient
  • Maggi Bhuna Masala Gravy- 1 pack.
  • Turmeric Powder- 1/2 teaspoon.
  • Red Chilli Powder- 1/2 teaspoon.
  • Coriander Powder- 1 teaspoon.
  • Peas- 200 grms, shelled.
  • Paneer- 300 grms, cut into cubes.
  • Water- 2 cups(300 ml).
  • Salt to taste.
  • Coriander leaves- 2 tablespoons, chopped.
Methods
  • Put the Maggi Bhuna Masala Gravy in a pressure cooker and stir-fry it for a minute. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and coriander powder. Fry for another minute.
  • Add peas and saute for 2-3 minutes. Then, add thepaneer cubes, water and salt to taste. Mix well. Cover the pressure cooker and cook for 2 minutes after the first whistle.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
