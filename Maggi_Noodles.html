Maggi Noodles
Ingredient
  • 1 Maggi 2 minute noodles (masala).
  • Oil- 1 tbsp.
  • Garlic- 6 cloves,crushed.
  • Black Cardamom- 1 tbsp, coarsely crushed.
  • Carrot- 1 cup, cut into thin juliennes.
  • Brocoli- 1 cup, small florets.
  • Baby Corn- 1 cup, thin round slices.
  • Spring Onins- 1 cup, sliced.
  • Water- 250 ml.
  • Salt to taste.
Methods
  • Heat oil,add garlic and saute till fragrant. Add black pepper and vegetables and stir fry till tender yet crunchy. now add salt to taste.
  • Prepare maggi 2 minute noodlesas per instructions on the pack and put it into the dish. Put the stir-fried vegetables over the noodles.
  • Serve hot.
